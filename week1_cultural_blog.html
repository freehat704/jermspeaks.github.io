Release 2: Why Music Matters

Music is important. Music has been with me throughout the vicissitudes of my life. It has gotten me through the zenith of my woes, carrying me on cross-country trips, and has allowed me to make some of my best friends. Each song has its own story to it, and it's like a separate memory for me, taking me back to some place I was before. I create music, and it becomes its own life when I give it a voice.

Release 4: Last time, my advice

I gave advice to my friend who was also going through a rough patch at work. I told them quitting my job was one of the best decisions of my life, and I won't ever have any regrets for doing it. However, the hard thing to swallow is the relationships you've built up over the span of your time there, and just seeing it go away, like breaking up with your first partner.
